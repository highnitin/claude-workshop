From download to first run

Set up your whole OS
in under ten minutes.

Install once inside a Claude Project. Every brand you work on gets its own Project, and your research, audits, and roadmaps persist across every session.

Step 1 of 3

Create a Claude Project

Open Claude, click + New Project, and name it 1MAP Performance OS. This is the home of your operating system.

Step 2 of 3

Upload the 7 skills

In the Project, open Skills → Add skill and upload all seven .md files. Confirm they appear in the list.

Step 3 of 3

Enable your connectors

Turn on Meta, Google, Apify, or Higgsfield as you need them. Missing a connector? The skills degrade gracefully — nothing breaks.

Don't run all seven on day one

Your first five weeks,
one real brand.

The recommended first cycle paces the skills so you learn the system as you go — and finish a full flywheel loop on a live account.

Week 1

Run Stages 1 and 2 on one existing brand you already know.

Week 2

Gaps + Opportunities

Turn the audit into a ranked 30/60/90 roadmap with Stages 3 and 4.

Week 3

Execution

Pick the top opportunity and build paused draft campaigns with Stage 5.

Weeks 4–5

Evaluate + Report

Let it run, then close the loop with Stages 6 and 7. One full cycle, done.
